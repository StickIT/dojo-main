Stéphan Euthine

 

Team LDLC co founder and LDLC Event co owner, Stephan Euthine makes esport grow in France thanks to Groupe LDLC since 2008. Today, he works on developing tools with the same objective, esports growth. He also represents a precursor movement to French esports associative project by being involved with France Esports. He will speak up during the workshops related to the teams and players.

Koen Schobbers

 

Founding member of the athletes commission of the International eSports Federation. Thanks to his experience as a professional esport athlete, he spreads his esports and sport enthusiasm as a TV host for Inside Esports broadcasted on FOX-Sports. Based in Netherlands, Koen Schobbers will speak up during the workshops related to the players as well as the Olympism.

Benoit Pagotto

 

Head of marketing at Fnatic, Benoit will be present at the Dojo Esport to speak up during the workshops about players and teams as well as the innovation. His experience within one of the most iconic teams in Europe is essential to think about esports future.

Sophia Metz

 

Founder of the Meltdown Esports Bars, created back in 2012, she now counts 25 structures around Europe and North America as well as an esports team. Sophia Metz has developed a well known brand into esports. Her experience and vision of the esports market will be an asset to our conference: “We found the ROI!”.

Jan Pommer

 

Jan Pommer is Turtle Entertainment Director of Germany where he takes care of teams and federations relations. His presence to the Dojo Esport offers both experience and an international point of view. He will speak up during the soft power conference as well as the Olympism workshop.

Jean-Baptiste Desmaizieres

 

France Marketing manager of Ubisoft, he has been part of the video game market since the beginning of his career. His knowledge will be put to use during the workshop about the Olympism as well as the soft power conference.

Olivier Ozoux

 

aAa Gaming president since 2014, historical structure founded back in 2000, Olivier will be a proud representant of the associative movement during the Dojo Esport. He will participate during the workshops dedicated to the associations and we be the link between local associations and city of Paris representants.

Jean-François Martins

 

He has been working for the city of Pairs since the 2010s. Now mayor adjunct in charge of sports and tourism since 2014, it is an honor to see him participate in the Dojo as a speaker during the introduction of the day but also during the workshop about Olympism.

Nicolas Weisz

 

Nicolas Weisz has built his experience between France and the USA within international renown lawyer’s offices. His expertise of employment rights and professional sports make him work with players / clubs / agents. During the Dojo Esport, he will bring his knowledge to the players / teams workshop.

Carson Knuth

 

Carson Knuth is the co owner of Rogue. His presence to the Dojo Esport offers the point of view of a business owner of one of the best organizations in the world. He will speak up during the soft power conference as well as the Teams workshops.
